LEAVE POLICY

Purpose
The leave policy is designed to support employee well-being while ensuring business continuity.

Casual Leave
Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves per calendar year.
Casual leave should be requested at least one day in advance whenever possible.

Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 sick leaves per year.
Medical documentation may be required for absences longer than three consecutive days.

Earned Leave
Employees receive 20 earned leaves per year.
Unused earned leaves can be carried forward up to a maximum of 30 days.

Maternity Leave
Eligible employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave.

Paternity Leave
Eligible employees are entitled to 10 days of paid paternity leave.

Bereavement Leave
Employees may take up to 5 days of leave in the event of the death of an immediate family member.

Leave Approval
All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal and approved by the reporting manager.

Leave Without Pay
If all eligible leave balances are exhausted, employees may apply for leave without pay subject to management approval.
