EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK

Welcome to ABC Global Technologies.

Company Overview
ABC Global Technologies is a multinational organization committed to innovation, collaboration, and employee well-being.

Working Hours
Employees are expected to work 8 hours per day, Monday through Friday.
Standard office hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM with a one-hour lunch break.

Code of Conduct
Employees must maintain professionalism, integrity, and respect in all workplace interactions.
Harassment, discrimination, and unethical behavior are strictly prohibited.

Performance Reviews
Performance evaluations are conducted twice a year.
Employees receive feedback on productivity, teamwork, and professional development.

Training and Development
The company provides access to technical training, leadership programs, and skill development workshops.

Employee Benefits
Employees are eligible for health insurance, paid leave, travel reimbursement, and work-from-home benefits according to company policies.

Resignation Policy
Employees must provide a minimum notice period of 30 days before leaving the organization.

Equal Opportunity Employment
The company provides equal opportunities regardless of gender, race, religion, age, or disability.
