WORK FROM HOME POLICY

Purpose
The company supports flexible work arrangements through its work-from-home program.

Eligibility
Employees who have completed six months of service are eligible to apply for work-from-home arrangements.

Work From Home Frequency
Employees may work from home up to three days per week with manager approval.

Working Hours
Employees must remain available during standard business hours from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Communication Requirements
Employees must attend virtual meetings and remain accessible through email, chat, and phone.

Equipment
The company provides laptops and approved software required for remote work.

Data Security
Employees must use company-approved VPN connections and follow cybersecurity guidelines.

Performance Expectations
Work-from-home employees are expected to maintain the same productivity and quality standards as office-based employees.

Revocation of Privilege
The company reserves the right to revoke work-from-home privileges if performance expectations are not met.
